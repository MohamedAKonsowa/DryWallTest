Common Questions

Louisville Drywall & Painting LLC FAQ

What areas do you serve?

We serve Louisville and surrounding areas, plus rural communities across Kentucky and Southern Indiana. If you're not sure whether we cover your town, contact us with your location and we'll let you know.

How far will your team travel for a job?

We travel throughout the Louisville metro area and to rural towns in Kentucky and Southern Indiana. Call (502) 546-2608 or send your town through the contact form — we'll confirm whether we can come to you.

What drywall services do you offer?

We offer drywall installation, repair, finishing, interior painting, and room remodel work for rural homes.

Are your rates affordable?

Yes. We're a small local crew with low overhead, so we can keep labor rates fair and affordable. We provide a free on-site estimate with a clear quote before any work starts. Repair and custom jobs are quoted separately based on the job.

Do I buy my own materials?

Yes. You purchase your own drywall, joint compound, tape, primer, and paint. We charge for labor only — that keeps your total cost down and lets you choose the products you want.

How do I get a free drywall estimate?
