Common Questions

Louisville Drywall & Painting LLC FAQ

Do you work in rural areas of Kentucky and Indiana?

Yes. Louisville Drywall & Painting LLC is a small team that travels to rural homes and small towns across Kentucky and Southern Indiana for drywall and painting work.

How far will your team travel for a job?

We serve rural communities throughout Kentucky and Southern Indiana. Contact us with your location and we'll let you know if we can make it out to you.

What drywall services do you offer?

We offer drywall installation, repair, finishing, interior painting, and room remodel work for rural homes.

How much do you charge?

Drywall installation: $5 per square foot (labor). Interior painting: $7 per square foot (labor). Repair and other work are quoted separately.

Do I buy my own materials?

Yes. You purchase your own drywall, joint compound, tape, primer, and paint. We charge for labor only — that keeps your costs clear and lets you choose the products you want.
